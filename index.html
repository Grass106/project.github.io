01 — The Problem

A gap in
everyday trust.

Many existing assistive technologies can provide information — but they're often difficult to use reliably in daily life. Some produce too much unnecessary output. Others aren't precise enough to build real confidence.

"The tools exist, but trusting them in the moment is a different thing entirely."
Composite insight from user interviews

One of the strongest and most consistent findings across all interviews involved liquid handling — pouring, measuring, and managing liquids accurately in everyday situations. Current solutions like measuring cups or touch-based estimation are unreliable and still require users to interpret information themselves.

03 — The Solution

One action.
One precise amount.

Instead of helping users estimate or measure liquids manually, the design shifted to direct liquid output control. The device automatically dispenses a fixed, precise amount of liquid — removing the need for any interpretation or judgment from the user.

Inspired by the familiar squeeze of a medicine bottle, the interaction is immediate and intuitive. No displays, no audio distractions, no guesswork.

◎

Precise Fixed Output

Dispenses exact amounts — 5ml or 10ml — automatically stopping after each dose. No measuring required.

◈

Squeeze Interaction

Modeled on the familiar feel of a medicine bottle — one squeeze, one dose. Instantly learnable.

◉

Spill Prevention

Vibration alerts notify the user when the receiving container is close to full — before any spill occurs.

◐

Non-Audio Feedback

No sounds. Feedback is tactile only, preserving the user's ability to hear their environment clearly.

◑

Locking Mechanism

Prevents accidental activation — the device only dispenses when intentionally triggered.

◒

Consistent Output

Every dispense is identical. Reliability builds trust — users always know exactly what they're getting.

I

Minimal

Avoid unnecessary complexity. Every feature must earn its place. If it doesn't help, it doesn't belong.

II

Precise

Exact, repeatable liquid control every time. Precision is the whole point — it cannot be compromised.

III

Reliable

Users should be able to reach for this device without thinking twice. Trust is built through consistency.
